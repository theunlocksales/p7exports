Who We Are

Premium Source of Pure, Fresh & High-Quality Indian Products

Spice Emporium by P7 Global Trade is dedicated to sourcing, processing, and exporting the finest spices and dehydrated products from India. Based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat — the epicenter of India's dehydrated onion and garlic industry — we have unparalleled access to the highest quality raw materials and processing facilities in the country.

Our products are carefully selected from trusted farms across Gujarat, Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Kerala, and other key agricultural regions. Every product undergoes rigorous quality checks including moisture analysis, microbiology testing, and purity verification to meet the highest international food safety standards.

Whether you are a food manufacturer, restaurant chain, spice brand, retail distributor, or wholesale buyer, P7 Global Trade provides a reliable, quality-assured supply of premium Indian products at competitive prices with complete export documentation and logistics support.

  • ✓ Private labeling and custom packaging available
