About P7 Global Trade

We Deliver The True Essence of Indian Flavor

P7 Global Trade is a premium merchant importer and exporter headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat — the heart of India's spice and dehydrated products industry. We specialize in sourcing, processing, and exporting the finest quality spices and dehydrated products to international markets.

Our products are carefully sourced from trusted farms across Gujarat, Maharashtra, Rajasthan, and other key producing regions. We work directly with farmers and processing units to ensure consistent quality, competitive pricing, and reliable supply throughout the year.

  • ✓ Direct sourcing from India's finest farms in Gujarat, Maharashtra & Rajasthan
  • ✓ State-of-the-art hygienically processed in modern FSSAI-compliant facilities
  • ✓ Serving food manufacturers, HoReCa, retail brands & distributors globally
  • ✓ Flexible MOQ, custom packaging, and private labeling options
  • ✓ Complete export documentation, phytosanitary certificates & COA provided

How We Work

Simple Export Process

From inquiry to delivery, our streamlined 4-step process ensures a smooth and hassle-free export experience for all our global partners.

01
📋

Inquiry & Quotation

Share your product requirements via WhatsApp, email, or our inquiry form. We respond with competitive pricing within 24 hours including product specs and certifications.

02
✅

Sample & Approval

We send product samples for your quality evaluation. Once approved, we finalize order quantity, packaging specifications, payment terms, and delivery timeline.

03
